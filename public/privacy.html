Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

At SnapLoad ("we", "us", "our"), your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at snapload.vercel.app (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

SnapLoad is designed to be privacy-respecting. We collect minimal data:

  • Usage Data: Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time of visit) for security and performance monitoring. These are not linked to any personal identity.
  • Video URLs: URLs you paste into the downloader are processed in real-time to retrieve your video. We do not store, log, or share these URLs after processing.
  • Cookies: We may use analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. We also display advertisements via Google AdSense which may use cookies.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To monitor usage and detect abuse or security issues
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To improve our Service over time

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregated data (e.g. total visitor counts) for reporting purposes.

Video download requests are proxied through cobalt.tools, an open-source service. Please review cobalt's own privacy policy for details on how they handle request data.

5. Data Retention

We do not store video URLs or downloaded media files. Server access logs are retained for up to 30 days for security purposes and then deleted.

6. Children's Privacy

SnapLoad is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have rights including: access to your data, correction of inaccurate data, deletion of your data, and objection to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@snapload.app
